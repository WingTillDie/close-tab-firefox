Makes Firefox touch-friendly by adding close tab button to toolbar. You can do following steps to make Firefox touch-friendly:
  1. Customize toolbar to move open new tab button next to URL
  2. Use Close Tab Button (this add-on)
  3. Use Undo Close Tab
